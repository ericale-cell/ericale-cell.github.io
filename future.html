Future Plans

I explored a multitude of classes in high school that ranged from advanced science concepts to computer languages to business and accounting principles. As I am currently learning about networks and the use of technology in business analysis, I am confident that I have found my passion. In order to achieve these future plans, I will continue to expand my knowledge in computer programming languages, such as C# and SQL, in addition to my basic knowledge of JavaScript and HTML. I will also work hard towards developing my skills of leadership, problem solving, and attention to detail. This summer, I plan to have an internship in which I can gain real-world experience in data and analytics.
